**Streamdal** is an _open-source data_ observability _and governance tool_ built on a collection of several **Opensource** [components](/en/core-components/overview) - A [server](/en/core-components/server), an [sdk](/en/core-components/sdk), and a [Console UI](/en/core-components/console-ui) - that integrate seamlessly into your code!


## Features
<ul>
<li style="padding: 5px 0px 5px 0px;">**Data <a href="/en/resources-support/glossary#observability" class="pkeyword">Observability</a>**: Think `tail -f` for your data!</li>
<li style="padding: 5px 0px 5px 0px;">**Data [Quality Monitoring](/en/data-governance/overview/)**: Ensuring your data's accuracy.</li>
<li style="padding: 5px 0px 5px 0px;"><img src={beta} alt="Beta" style="display: inline" /> **Data Governance & Enforcement**: Keeping your data compliant.</li>
<li style="padding: 5px 0px 5px 0px;"><img src={beta} alt="Beta" style="display: inline" /> **Data Transformation**: Tailoring your data.</li>
<li style="padding: 5px 0px 5px 0px;"><img src={beta} alt="Beta" style="display: inline" /> **Data Obfuscation, Masking, and Stripping**: Securing your data.</li>
</ul>

## SDK
Connect your applications up to Streamdal via the SDK. Below is instrumentation guide and language sepcific SDKs we currently offer with more to come:

- **Instrumentation Guide**: Start here [View Instrumentation Guide](/en/guides/instrumentation)
- **Go SDK**: Enhance your Go applications with Streamdal. [View Go SDK Docs](/en/sdks/go)
- **Node SDK**: Integrate Streamdal into your Node.js projects. [View Node SDK Docs](/en/sdks/node)
- **Python SDK**: Implement Streamdal functionalities in your Python applications. [View Python SDK Docs](/en/sdks/python)
