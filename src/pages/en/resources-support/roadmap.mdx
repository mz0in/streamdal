Our roadmap can be found publicly on 
[Github](https://github.com/orgs/streamdal/projects/1).

Before opening a new issue or discussion, be sure to search for existing topics 
and read about [contributing to Snitch](/en/resources-support/contributing). 
For everything else, like asking quick questions, participating in polls, 
learning about edge use cases, and common challenges or implementations, you 
can get access to most of these on our 
[community discord](/en/resources-support/community).
