There are a variety of ways to be notified, or alerted, that a monitor has
triggered. Alerts are a necessary step when setting up
[monitors](/en/components/monitor/overview/#alerts).

We currently support alerting through:

- Slack
- E-Mail
- PagerDuty

### Setting up Alerts

To set up a new alert, navigate to the alerts page and select `+Add new alert`.
From here, fill in the appropriate fields.
