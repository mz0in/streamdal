Snitch is primarily used to provide real-time validation and integrity for any 
data system. It will deliver preventive and proactive measures for data 
governance and compliance initiatives. 

Whether you're ensuring [data quality](/en/data-governance/data-quality), 
acquiring new or fortifying existing compliance certifications, or enforcing 
[internal data governance](/en/data-governance/internal-governance) policies 
for your organization or business partners, Snitch will drive consistency and 
protection wherever data moves.

### Data Compliance & Certifications

The lists below will continue to grow as more use cases surface and case 
studies complete.

[Let us know](/en/resources-support/get-support/) so we can clarify 
applicability, and add it to the list or roadmap._
